Child Safety and CSAE Policy

Last updated: December 2025

Our Commitment to Child Safety

Poll Zero is committed to protecting children and maintaining a safe, respectful, and lawful platform. We have zero tolerance for any form of Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) on our platform.

We actively work to prevent, detect, remove, and report any content or behavior that involves or promotes CSAE.

Prohibited Content and Behavior

The following are strictly prohibited on Poll Zero:

Any sexual content involving minors

Any depiction, discussion, or encouragement of child sexual abuse or exploitation

Grooming behaviors or attempts to solicit minors

Sharing, requesting, or linking to CSAE material

Any content that sexualizes a minor in any form

This applies to all content types including posts, comments, messages, images, videos, links, and profiles.

Prevention and Moderation Measures

We use a combination of the following measures to enforce child safety:

Clear content guidelines and community standards

User reporting mechanisms for inappropriate content or behavior

Manual moderation and review of reported content

Immediate action including content removal and account suspension when violations are found

Accounts found engaging in CSAE related activity are permanently banned.

Reporting and Law Enforcement Cooperation

Any confirmed CSAE content or activity is handled with the highest priority.

Content is immediately removed from the platform

Relevant accounts are permanently disabled

Where legally required, we cooperate with appropriate law enforcement authorities

We comply with applicable child protection laws and regulations

User Reporting

Users can report content or users directly within the app.
Reports related to child safety are prioritized and reviewed urgently.

If you believe a child is in immediate danger, please contact local emergency services.
