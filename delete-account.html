Delete Your Poll Zero Account

You can permanently delete your Poll Zero account and associated personal data at any time.

How to delete your account

  1. Open the Poll Zero app
  2. Go to Profile → Settings
  3. Select “Delete Account”
  4. Confirm deletion

Once confirmed, your account and personal data will be scheduled for deletion.

If you cannot access the app

If you are unable to log in, you can request account deletion by emailing us from your registered email address.

Email: hello@zerosocial.app
Subject: Delete my Poll Zero account

We may verify ownership before processing the request.
